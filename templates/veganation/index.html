Vegan Tips & Facts!

  • Each vegan saves 30 animal lives per year!
  • Pursuing a vegan diet can cut your carbon footprint in half - animal agriculture is a huge contributor to the issue of climate change.
  • On average, vegans have a longer life span - it can add up to 4 year onto your life!
  • A vegan saves approximately 1,1000 gallons of water per day - the meat industry watses 1/3 of drinkable water!
  • Vegans are less likely to have a stroke or get heart disease.
  • A plant based diet increases your metabolism!
  • The term 'Vegan' was coined less than 100 years go - animal rights activist David Watson created the word in the 1940s.
